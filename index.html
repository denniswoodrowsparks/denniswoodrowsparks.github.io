About Dennis

Dennis Sparks was born in Charlotte, NC but grew up in the Greater Atlanta, GA area. He spent much of his time playing soccer, participating in the highest levels of the game through high school. During his college years, Dennis joined The Ohio State University Men's Soccer Program where he was a part of the 2015 Big Ten Championship team that also made a run to the quarterfinals of the NCAA tournament. Upon graduating in 2017, Dennis Sparks went on to continue his education and soccer career at Trevecca Nazarene University, a Division II program in Nashville, TN.

Dennis holds a B.S. in Computer Engineering from Ohio State and an M.B.A with a focus in Information Technology from Trevecca. Dennis' professional interests include Health Care Integration, Database Administration and Development, Java Programming, Python Programming, Software Implementation, and Business Analysis. Dennis has developed and worked numerous systems in the Health Care and Retail industries. His coursework and experience in Software and Business have given him a unique perspective when solving probelms.

In his free time, Dennis enjoys writing, playing soccer, and purusing his own personal development.
